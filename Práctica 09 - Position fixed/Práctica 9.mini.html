Bandcamp Weekly We Got the Jazz

Check out big, electronic tunes by Cotton Claw, Maribou State, and Braille, plus the latest from LA producer Nosaj Thing and soul singer Andreya Triana. Pianist Marc Cary talks about a new album and go-go documentary, and Gerald of Jazzman Records chats about the joys and woes of reissuing rare records.

Hosted by Andrew Jervis. Illustration of Marc Cary by Paul Grelet.
